# Privacy Policy for MR.CV

**Effective Date: October 2023**

This Privacy Policy describes how **MR.CV** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a professional CV building experience, we collect the following information:
*   **Account Information**: Email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.
*   **CV Data**: Information you provide for your CV, including full name, job title, contact details (phone, address), education history, work experience, skills, projects, and social profile links (GitHub, LinkedIn).
*   **AI Processing Data**: The CV data you provide is sent to **Google Gemini AI** to generate or analyze your resume.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
*   Create and manage your user account.
*   Store and synchronize your CVs across devices using Firebase Firestore.
*   Generate professional resume content using AI.
*   Analyze your CV to provide success scores and improvement tips.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. However, we use the following third-party services:
*   **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication and database storage.
*   **Google Gemini AI**: For content generation and analysis.
*   **PDF/Printing Libraries**: To process documents locally on your device.

## 4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Firebase provides industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information stored in the cloud.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time directly through the app's editing and deletion features.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/motaz-almomani/cv_project
