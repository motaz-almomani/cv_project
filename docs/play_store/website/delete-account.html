Account & data deletion

You can ask us to delete your MR.CV account and the personal data we hold through Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from the same email address registered on your MR.CV account.
  2. Use subject line: MR.CV — Account deletion request.
  3. State clearly that you want your account and associated data deleted. If you no longer have access to the registered email, describe how we can reasonably verify your identity.

What we remove from Firebase

After verifying your request, we will:

  • Delete your Firebase Authentication user record (sign-in identifiers for that account).
  • Delete documents in Cloud Firestore tied to your user ID — for MR.CV, résumés are stored under the cvs collection and associated with your userId; these records will be deleted or anonymised according to operational procedure.

Operational backups may retain fragments for a short period pursuant to Firebase/Google retention; after routine backup rotation, deletion is expected to be irreversible beyond standard disaster-recovery timelines.

Deletion timeline

  • Confirmation: we aim to acknowledge your request within 7 business days.
  • Completion: we aim to complete account and primary data deletion within 30 days of verification, unless a legal exception applies or additional verification is needed.

Complex or disputed requests may require additional time — we will inform you.

Deletion on your device

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored preferences on most devices. Clearing app storage in Android settings achieves the same. This does not delete remote server data unless you delete your account or request deletion.

In-app deletion (recommended product improvement)

Google Play’s account deletion policy generally expects developers to provide an in-app pathway or a straightforward web/deep link where feasible. MR.CV’s baseline release described for this documentation may rely on email-based deletion unless you ship an in-account “Delete my account” action (calling Firebase Auth delete() plus cascading Firestore cleanup). Prefer adding in-app deletion and linking to this URL from Help/Settings.

Confirmation process

We verify ownership by confirming control of the account email or other reasonable verification. After deletion, we may send one final acknowledgment to the requesting address.
