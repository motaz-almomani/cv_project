Privacy Policy

MR.CV is operated by Developer Hub 899 (“we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app processes when you use our Android application built with Flutter, and how Google Firebase services are used.

1. Who we are

Developer Hub 899 publishes MR.CV, a résumé / CV builder and management app. The application uses Firebase Authentication for sign-in and Cloud Firestore to store your CV documents. Optional settings may be stored only on your device.

2. Data we collect and process

2.1 Account and authentication (Firebase Authentication)

  • Email address — used to create and sign in to your account.
  • User identifier (UID) — a unique ID generated by Firebase; we use it to associate your saved CVs with your account.
  • Display name — if you choose to set or update it in the app, it is stored in your Firebase user profile.
  • Authentication state — required to keep you signed in securely on your device.

2.2 Content you provide (Cloud Firestore)

When you create or edit a CV, we store the fields you enter (for example: name, job title, contact details, summary, experience, education, skills, projects, certificates, and related metadata such as CV title and timestamps). Documents are stored in Firestore and linked to your userId.

2.3 Device and local data

Some preferences may be stored locally on your device (for example via platform storage APIs). This data stays on the device unless you choose to synchronise equivalent information through your account elsewhere in the product.

2.4 Analytics

We aim to keep analytics minimal. The app is not marketed here as integrating Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics. If you add analytics SDKs later, update this policy and the Data safety declaration in Google Play Console to match reality.

3. Purposes of processing

  • Providing account registration, authentication, and session management.
  • Storing and displaying your résumés and related content.
  • Maintaining security and integrity (for example misuse prevention).
  • Fulfilling legal obligations where applicable.

4. Third-party services — Google Firebase

We use Firebase, a service operated by Google, which acts as infrastructure for authentication and database storage. Firebase’s handling of data is governed by Google’s policies and your Firebase/Google account terms.

  • Firebase Authentication — processes credentials and account tokens.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores structured data you save in the app.

We do not sell your personal information. Data is not shared with unrelated third-party advertisers solely through the default configuration described above. If you enable additional Firebase products or other SDKs, disclose them explicitly here and in Play Console.

5. Legal bases (where GDPR / UK GDPR applies)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the app), legitimate interests (security and abuse prevention), and, where required, your consent — for example for optional communications or optional features.

6. Data retention

  • Account and Firestore content — kept until you delete your account or ask us to delete your data, subject to reasonable backup or legal hold periods.
  • Deletion requests — described on our Account & data deletion page (for example completion within approximately 30 days where technically feasible).

7. Security measures

  • Communication with Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS).
  • Firestore and Auth data are protected by Google’s infrastructure; you should deploy Firestore Security Rules so only the owning user can read/write their documents.
  • We recommend a strong, unique password and keeping your device operating system updated.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children

MR.CV is not directed at children under the age required by your region (for example 13 in the United States, or 16 where stricter rules apply). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Firebase may process data in data centres in various regions according to your Firebase project settings and Google’s practices. Review Google/Firebase documentation for safeguards such as standard contractual clauses where applicable.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the bottom will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy, unless applicable law requires a different process.
